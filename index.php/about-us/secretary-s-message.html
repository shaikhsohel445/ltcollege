Secretary's Message

Since it's established in 1959 the MSP Mandal has strived hard and succeeded to achieve two objectives. The first is the spread of education to the door step of the poor and needful students; the second one is imparting high quality of education.

As result it is seen that the MSP Man`dal is a leading educational institute in both quality and quantity. Of late, the institute has introduced need based and employment oriented modern courses. After succeeding in imparting traditional education of high quality, now the mandal enters the field of professional and technical education.

 Yashwantrao Chavan Institute of Polytechnic, Beed is our first attempt to equip our student with solid technological base which definitely shape their career

 

Hon. Satish Bhanudasrao Chavan

Secretary
