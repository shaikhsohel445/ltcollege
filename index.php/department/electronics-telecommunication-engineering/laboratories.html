ELECTRICAL & ELECTRONICS ENGINEERING

Laboratories

+ - Electronic Devices & Circuits Laboratory : Click to collapse

Lab-Information: The objective of Power electronics laboratory is to conduct the practical’s of UG Course Electronics devices &circuit. The electronics practical provides from root level hardware. The Course Electronics devices &circuit Lab enhances the students by providing them the better understanding of concepts & working of advanced  semiconductor devices & electronics circuits.

Area (sq. m): 81.73 sq. m.

Major Equipment/Kit: 1. DC regulated power supply, 2. Opamp trainer kit 3. 20 MHz Dual Channel oscilloscope scope, 4. 1 MHz function generator etc.

Software Tools: Nil

No. of PC: Nil

Name of Lab In-charge: Prof. Jogdand S.V.

 

+ - Digital Integrating Circuit Lab : Click to collapse

Lab-Information: The objective of Digital Integrated Circuit  laboratory is to conduct the practical’s of UG Course digital logic design & digital electronics. Digital electronics is the foundation of computers and microprocessor-based systems found in automobiles, industrial control systems, and home entertainment systems. The Course  Digital Integrated circuit Lab aggrandizes the students by providing them the concepts logical programming &  working of advanced  Integrated Circuit like CPLD, PLA,etc.

Area (sq. m): 83.64 sq. m.

Major Equipment/Kit: 1. 16:1 Multiplexer trainer kit, 2. Breadboard trainer kit 3. Seven Segment Display Decoder, MUX & DEMUX Kit 4. Digital Trainer kit (20x4) with power cord ,etc.

Software Tools: Nil

No. of PC: Nil

Name of Lab In-charge: Prof. Wangikar V.V.

+ - Programming Lab : Click to collapse

Lab-Information: The objective of Programming laboratory is to conduct the software practical’s of UG Course Digital signal processing & electronics devices .  Programming is the is need of era & found in automobiles, industrial control systems, and home entertainment systems. The programming Course  prepares  the students to solve real time problem by programming.

Area (sq. m): 84.39 sq. m.

Major Equipment/Kit: 1. Stabilizer   2. LCD projector  3.UPS 4. Printer, etc.

Software Tools: multisim,Turbo c,Keil,Scilab

No. of PC: 20

Name of Lab In-charge: Prof. Dohare  B. N.

+ - Analog & Digital  communication Lab : Click to collapse

Lab-Information: The objective of Analog & Digital  communication  laboratory is to conduct the  practical of UG Course Analog communication system.  Analog communication system is seen in AM radio station & FM radio station. The analog communication Course  prepares  the students to understand  basic concepts in communication practically & to make them compatible with communication industry.

Area (sq. m): 77.29 sq. m.

Major Equipment/Kit: 1.DSB SC trainer kit  2.Amplitude Mod. Demodulation Experimental Panel 3.Microphone & Loudspeaker Trainer kit  4.SSB System trainer kit, etc.

Software Tools: nil

No. of PC: nil

Name of Lab In-charge: Prof. Nelwade S.D.

+ - Advance Communication Lab :  Click to collapse

Lab-Information: The objective of Advance Communication   laboratory is to conduct the  practical of UG Course wireless communication system. Advance communication system is used  in Military application, mobile communication, optical fiber communication. The Advance communication Course enhances the students by providing  practical knowledge & working of advanced  Communication circuits.

Area (sq. m): 77.29 sq. m.

Major Equipment/Kit: 1. Microwave Test bench with components 2. Optical power meter 3 Fiber optic analog & digital trainer kit   4. Microwave power meter,  etc.

Software Tools: nil

No. of PC: nil

Name of Lab In-charge: Prof. Nelwade S.D.

+ - Advanced Microprocessor Lab : Click to collapse

 

Lab-Information: The objective of Advanced Microprocessor laboratory is to conduct the practical of UG Course microprocessor interfacing.Advanced Microprocessor is used  in pentium processors, Celeron processors. The Advanced Microprocessor enhances  the students knowledge by providing concepts of advanced programming skills.

Area (sq. m): 80.36 sq. m.

Major Equipment/Kit: 1. Micro-Processor 8085 Trainer kit 2. Micro-Processor 8086 Trainer kit 3 Microcontroller trainer kit with device Interfacing    4. Microprocessor 8085 Interfacing Kit,  etc.

Software Tools: nil

No. of PC: nil

Name of Lab In-charge: Prof. Sayyad H.G.

+ - VLSI & Embedded System Lab : Click to collapse

 

Lab-Information: The objective of VLSI & Embedded   laboratory is to conduct the  simulation practical’s of UG Course VLSI & embedded system . The essence of VLSI and embedded is in practical product development, practical mindset is more important than the theoretical knowledge. The VLSI Course   prepares   the students to face industry environment in programming & hardware manufacturing.

Area (sq. m): 84.39 sq. m.

Major Equipment/Kit: 1.89c51RD2 Based development Board with System Programming  2.ARM7(LPC2148) development board  3.Atys spartan 6    4.Stabilizer, LCD projector ,UPS ,Printer, etc.

Software Tools: .multisim, Turbo c, Keil, Scilab, Xilinx

No. of PC: 20

Name of Lab In-charge: Prof. Dohare  B. N.

+ - Power Electronics Lab : Click to collapse

 

Lab-Information: The purpose  of Power electronics laboratory is to conduct the practical’s of UG Course Power Electronics. Main components of this Lab comprised of single Phase Half & Fully controlled converter, three phase Full converter, Speed Control of DC motor,20 MHz Dual Channel power scope. The Power Electronics Lab amplify  knowledge of  the students by providing them the better understanding of concepts & working of advanced power semiconductor devices & power electronics circuits.

Area (sq. m): 81.73 sq. m.

Major Equipment/Kit: 1. VI Characteristics of SCR,DIAC,TRIAC & MOSFET 2. Single Phase series Invertor

  1. Single phase cyclo converter, etc.

Software Tools: .nil

No. of PC: 00

Name of Lab In-charge: Prof. Jogdand S.V.

+ - Network & Measurement Lab : Click to collapse

 

Lab-Information: The Network & Measurement laboratory is  designed to conduct the practical’s of UG Course electronic measurement devices ,control system The laboratory is involved in all the areas of study, related to measurement and calibration. It is equipped with all measuring instruments, phase shifting transformers, bridge circuits and induction coil etc. Here students learn to calibrate the meters, verify theorems, and understand hysteresis characteristics & other important concepts related to measuring.

Area (sq. m): 70.62 sq. m.

Major Equipment/Kit: 1. Temperature PI/PID controller & MOSFET   2. CRO Trainer Kit

  1. Experimental kit for Wheatstone Bridge kit 4. DMM Demonstrator  etc.

Software Tools: nil

No. of PC: 00

Name of Lab In-charge: Prof. Wangikar V.V.
