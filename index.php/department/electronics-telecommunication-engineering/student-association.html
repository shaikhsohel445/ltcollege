ETSA COMMITTEE

A.Y. 2018-19

The  Electrical & Electronics Engineering student association  is established to bring about the technical development of students by organizing seminars, workshops and other activities .Our objective is  to  improve non-technical abilities of students by engendering good communication skills, managerial abilities, presentation skills and team work. The purpose is also to act as a forum for students to voice their opinions and suggestions.
