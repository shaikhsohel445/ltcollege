Our Library is well maintained and well furniture. We have about 11249 books, 1908 titles. More than 42 National and International Journals and periodicals are subscribed to currently which facilitates research in our college. We also have multiple copies of National, Regional and Local Newspapers and Magazines. Also we have E-Journals viz.,Del-Net. We have Book Bank scheme for toppers and other students.  The total management of Library is from MSPM_ERP software. We have a specious Library hall with separate reading room for boys and girls. The total environment in Library as well as reading room is very silent and useful for study.

Features :

  • Collection for Competitive Examinations like UPSC, MPSC, GATE, CAT, GRE etc.
  • Collection of Marathi books of renowned authors
  • Collection of books on Sports, Yoga etc.
  • Reference books available in the night library
  • Display of Newspaper
  • Night Library
  • Earn and learn facility
  • Information display and notification
